THE PROBLEM

At present, the ratio of high school tutors to students is very unbalanced. Only 473 students will have college application advisors. Students in public schools usually receive only half an hour of tutoring during their entire high school career. This is really surprising. Therefore, many parents are willing to pay high fees to hire private consultants to help their children complete the college application plan. However, the price is high. Not all families can afford this fee. So many children do not receive enough guidance before applying to college. They missed the university they could have entered. The cost of traditional college application consultants is very high, which is difficult for ordinary families to afford. This allows universities to apply for software. Suppose the effect can reach 80% of university application consultants. They should be interested in it.
