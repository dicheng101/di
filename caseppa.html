PlanMyCollege

WHAT

It is a consumer-oriented self-service platform for university planning and applications. It provides a full set of services for families who want to apply to colleges for their children, assess the competitiveness of high school students, create a personalized family action plan, design a balanced list of universities, and provide expert feedback on essays and applications.
