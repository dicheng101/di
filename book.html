WHAT

This is the project of book design. Make a book with specific natural phenomena of your choice. I really like the natural phenomenon of dawn, so I chose it. The best part of the whole design process is a huge imagination. Due to all the design requirements, you only know the word "dawn." Other pictures, text, and styles are all from your creativity. For me, this is a wonderful design experience.
