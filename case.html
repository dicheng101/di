E-Wallet

An e-wallet app that kids can easily save and know their money.

WHAT

An e-wallet app that kids can easily save and spend their money.An e-wallet app that parents can teach children about money by giving them money

HOW

Parents give their children pocket money through e-wallets. Children need to apply with their parents before they want to buy something. Once the parents agree, they will purchase for the child, and the corresponding amount will deduct from the child‘s wallet.
